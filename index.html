About Me

25+ Years Creating

I've spent my career in the visual effects industry, primarily as a compositor and matte painter, contributing to film and television projects that demanded both technical precision and artistic vision. Alongside my visual work, I've been composing music and designing sound for just as long, exploring the intersection of audio and image to create truly immersive experiences.

My creative practice extends across VFX, 3D art, animation, photography, video editing, music production, and sound design. Whether I'm painting a digital matte, sculpting a soundscape, or writing a melody, I approach every project with the same philosophy: let the technology serve the creative vision.

On the technical side, I'm drawn to creative coding, building custom tools and scripts that streamline workflows and open up new creative possibilities. I can handle web development when needed, but my real passion lies in writing code that empowers artists and unlocks new forms of expression, whether that's pipeline tools, generative art, or interactive experiences.
